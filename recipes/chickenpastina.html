Chicken Pastina

Chef John's chicken pastina is like a big, warm hug from an Italian grandma, a comfort food that will, quite literally, change your mood.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups prepared chicken broth
  • salt to taste
  • 1 cup stelline/stelle (star shaped) pastina, or other pastina such as acini di pepe
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup finely grated parmigiano reggiano cheese (1 1/2 ounces by weight)
  • extra-virgin olive oil
